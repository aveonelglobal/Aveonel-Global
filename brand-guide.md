## Brand Voice Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Speak directly ("I" not "we") | Use corporate jargon |
| Be warm and reassuring | Be salesy or pushy |
| Focus on peace of mind | Only talk about features |
| Share personal stories | Be generic |
| Use simple language | Overcomplicate |
